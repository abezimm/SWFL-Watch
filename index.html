About Us

When you’re out of town, you don’t want to have to worry about what’s going on at home. We don’t think you should have to, either. That’s why we’ve created SWFL Watch, LLC. House or condo, first home or second, regular snowbird or occasional traveler--we’ll watch out for you.

We are a family owned and operated company with independence in mind. Independence for us to live the life we want in paradise and independence for you to come and go as you please without all the stress about what’s going on in your home while you’re gone. We are licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of Florida.

When you deal with SWFL Watch LLC, you get the personal attention only a small business can provide. You’ll only speak to one of two people--Abe or Jess. This means that once we’re acquainted, we’ll know who you are and be ready to assist you in the best way possible.
